Why I made this blog

With the rich and often untold history of moderns day commonly used utilities such as blender, this site not only provides the history but also the personal commentary of someone who has lived through and watched the growth firsthand. in doing this, the hope thta current, possibly ignored utilities and projects get the recognition as possible future fruit, that they may otherwise never have.
