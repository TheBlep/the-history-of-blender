Blender itself!

Blender is a free and open source program. Think of the analogy; a swiss army knife is good at many things but perfect at none. This used to be what Blender was in the 3D space. now with the ammount of funding and attention it has recieved in the past years, it has not only retained its swiss army knife utility but sharpened its proverbial knife. It has become so wel known that even mercedes (among many other famous brands) use it for 3D marketing and product design.

The learning curve

The second most well known thing about Blender is its notorouse learning curve. Difficult to learn at first but outstanding and long time use once the pain ebbs (Quite similar to a good pair of new boots) here are some links and advice that helped me learn it many years ago and to newer resources that I beleive are priceless in helping those new to Blender get up to speed fast.
